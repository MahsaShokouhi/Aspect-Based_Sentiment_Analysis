Restaurant Review Sentiment Analysis



To analyze a multi-aspect review, it is first split into different phrases, each corresponding to a specific component of the services provided by a restaurant.

For each phrase, the general topic (aspect) discussed, its sentiment (positive/negative/neutral), the subject of discussion, and the specific descriptors used by the reviewer are extracted.
